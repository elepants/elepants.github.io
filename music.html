my music

what is beepbox?

This is my music archive. I have been making digital music since 2021, primarily using Jummbus/Beepbox.

Beepbox is an online tool for sketching music. I began with an intention to make a resource pack for my friend's Minecraft server. Over the years, I've amassed a giant disorganized list to completed songs. That's why I created this website!


→Try it for yourself here!←

Somehow, in the never-silent chaos of my internal monologue, these melodies rummage their way to the forefront of my brain. After I find myself whistling them enough to get the tune stuck, I sketch them into Beepbox and develop them into songs!
