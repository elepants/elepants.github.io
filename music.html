My music ! !

This is my music archive! I use this online browser called "Jummbox" to make music. It is a modified version of the original "Beepbox", created by John Nesky. It's a free, simple, and intuitive site that lets you make music note-by-note, and play it in real time. I've found it a very satisfying creative outlet to experiment with melodies I find myself whistling, and this site lets me develop them into something more.

I started out with this around 2021 with the intention of making a resource pack for the minecraft server my friends and I share, but over time I've found myself with a folder of links to completed songs I don't know where to put. That's what this page is for!
