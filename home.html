These are my internet archives. I do a lot of things on my 'puter, so this is the site I built to house everything. This site was a challenge to myself to see if I could actually learn how to do it.

I am coding this entire website from scratch with HTML and CSS via replit.
I hope you enjoy rummaging through my stuff as much as I enjoy putting it all together.

Note: I am not a graphic designer, so sorry if anything is formatted terribly or is ugly. You can send your grievances here.

I use an online browser called Jummbox to make music. It's a free and intuitive site created by John Nesky that lets you make music note-by-note, and play it in real time.

I started out in 2021 intending to make a resource pack for the minecraft server my friends and I shared. Over time, I've found myself with a huge, disorganized folder of links to completed songs.

One of the primary reasons I made this website was to house all of my music, and to make it easier to share.
