Lunch with the Blind Woman
Night of June 28, 2022.

I was a younger version of myself, young enough to be accompanied everywhere by my mom, who was also younger. But it was fun. I enjoyed my time with her, and one of the places we went on our special day (I didn’t know what the occasion was!) was the house of a slightly older woman with blonde hair and a kind face. She was blind, or at least that’s what I was told. It was her birthday today! My mom and I came to bring her a special treat; a little platter of hors d'oeuvres. We knew she would love them. While the woman didn’t look like her, she reminded me a lot of Ms. A. She was great. We sat down in her house, which was also some kind of Mediterranean restaurant of some sort, but only in the dining room.

At some point in the dream, I had some anxiety over getting to a softball game. I wasn’t even sure if I had a game that day, but I looked down and realized I was in my old purple uniform. I had my braided pigtails with ribbons tied at the ends. I needed to make sure I didn’t miss my game. I took out my phone and called Emily Farthing. She would know, for sure.

The phone call never went through, but I didn’t really worry about it. I remembered I was a kid, so worrying was my mom’s job. While we hung out with the blind woman, we took a seat at the “restaurant” table, which was just a platform slightly above the ground, like a chabudai. We sat and talked, and after a while we ordered a few bites from the menu, but not too much! (because I had a softball game right after this) It’s early afternoon/late morning. Mom says we can come back after for a true lunch, but to just get a bite now. This plan was foiled as the restaurant staff accidentally brought us full plates of food. I stuffed as much shawarma in my face that I could before we left but I’m still hungry. I’m just glad we would come back after.

I wake up to the sound of someone whispering my name. Over and over. At least ten times. The first was the loudest and clearest, and after the second or third I was fully awake. But the whisper got quieter and quieter, but still clearly whispering my name. The voice was nondescript, I couldn’t tell if it was male or female. Honestly, it kind of sounded like my voice. The more I focused on it, the more the voice seemed to intentionally get quieter. I could hear my heart pounding so fast; I became wide awake very quickly, really scared. It was 7:20AM.
